# Introduction to Disorders Affecting Multiple Body Systems of Cats
By[Dana G. Allen](), DVM, MSc, DACVIM, Ontario Veterinary College
Reviewed/Revised Aug 2018 | Modified Sept 2024
v3247792
There are many disorders that can affect multiple parts of the body. These may be caused , viruses, poisonous or toxic substances in the environment, and other health hazards. Disorders affecting multiple body systems can also be inherited or develop while the animal is still in the womb. Diseases or conditions that involve multiple organ systems may also be described as systemic or generalized.
