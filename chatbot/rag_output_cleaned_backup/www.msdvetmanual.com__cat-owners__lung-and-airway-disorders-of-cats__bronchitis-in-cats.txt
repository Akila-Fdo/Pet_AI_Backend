# Bronchitis in Cats
ByNed F. Kuehn, DVM, MS, DACVIM, Michigan Veterinary Specialists
Bronchitis is an inflammation of the bronchial airways that may extend into the lungs. It is discussed later in this chapter as part of tracheobronchitis.
