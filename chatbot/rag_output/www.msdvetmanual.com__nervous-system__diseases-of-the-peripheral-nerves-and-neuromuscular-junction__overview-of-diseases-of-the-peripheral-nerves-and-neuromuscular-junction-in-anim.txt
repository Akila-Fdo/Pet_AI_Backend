# Overview of Diseases of the Peripheral Nerves and Neuromuscular Junction in Animals
By[William B. Thomas](https://www.msdvetmanual.com/authors/thomas-william), DVM, DACVIM-Neurology, Small Animal Neurology, College of Veterinary Medicine, University of Tennessee
Reviewed/Revised May 2021 | Modified Sept 2024
v3287638
Diseases of the peripheral nerves and neuromuscular junction include [degenerative diseases](https://www.msdvetmanual.com/nervous-system/diseases-of-the-peripheral-nerves-and-neuromuscular-junction/degenerative-disorders-of-the-peripheral-nerves-and-neuromuscular-junction-in-animals), [inflammatory diseases](https://www.msdvetmanual.com/nervous-system/diseases-of-the-peripheral-nerves-and-neuromuscular-junction/inflammatory-disorders-of-the-peripheral-nerves-and-neuromuscular-junction-in-animals), [metabolic disorders](https://www.msdvetmanual.com/nervous-system/diseases-of-the-peripheral-nerves-and-neuromuscular-junction/metabolic-disorders-of-the-peripheral-nerves-and-neuromuscular-junction-in-animals), [neoplasia](https://www.msdvetmanual.com/nervous-system/diseases-of-the-peripheral-nerves-and-neuromuscular-junction/neoplasia-of-the-peripheral-nerves-and-neuromuscular-junction-in-animals), [nutritional disorders](https://www.msdvetmanual.com/nervous-system/diseases-of-the-peripheral-nerves-and-neuromuscular-junction/nutritional-disorders-of-the-peripheral-nerves-and-neuromuscular-junction-in-animals), [toxic disorders](https://www.msdvetmanual.com/nervous-system/diseases-of-the-peripheral-nerves-and-neuromuscular-junction/toxic-disorders-of-the-peripheral-nerves-and-neuromuscular-junction-in-animals), [trauma](https://www.msdvetmanual.com/nervous-system/diseases-of-the-peripheral-nerves-and-neuromuscular-junction/trauma-of-the-peripheral-nerves-and-neuromuscular-junction-in-animals), and [vascular diseases](https://www.msdvetmanual.com/nervous-system/diseases-of-the-peripheral-nerves-and-neuromuscular-junction/vascular-diseases-of-the-peripheral-nerves-and-neuromuscular-junction-in-animals), some of which are [congenital disorders](https://www.msdvetmanual.com/nervous-system/congenital-and-inherited-anomalies-of-the-nervous-system-in-small-animals).
