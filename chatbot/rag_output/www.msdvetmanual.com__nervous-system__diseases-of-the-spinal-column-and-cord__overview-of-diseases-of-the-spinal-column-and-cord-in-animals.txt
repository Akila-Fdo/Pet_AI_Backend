# Overview of Diseases of the Spinal Column and Cord in Animals
By[William B. Thomas](https://www.msdvetmanual.com/authors/thomas-william), DVM, DACVIM-Neurology, Small Animal Neurology, College of Veterinary Medicine, University of Tennessee
Reviewed/Revised Oct 2021 | Modified Sept 2024
v3287879
Diseases of the spinal column and cord include congenital disorders, [degenerative diseases](https://www.msdvetmanual.com/nervous-system/diseases-of-the-spinal-column-and-cord/degenerative-diseases-of-the-spinal-column-and-cord-in-animals), [inflammatory and infectious diseases](https://www.msdvetmanual.com/nervous-system/diseases-of-the-spinal-column-and-cord/inflammatory-and-infectious-diseases-of-the-spinal-column-and-cord-in-animals), [neoplasia](https://www.msdvetmanual.com/nervous-system/diseases-of-the-spinal-column-and-cord/neoplasia-of-the-spinal-column-and-cord-in-animals), [nutritional disorders](https://www.msdvetmanual.com/nervous-system/diseases-of-the-spinal-column-and-cord/nutritional-disorders-of-the-spinal-column-and-cord-in-animals), [trauma](https://www.msdvetmanual.com/nervous-system/diseases-of-the-spinal-column-and-cord/trauma-of-the-spinal-column-and-cord-in-animals), [toxic disorders](https://www.msdvetmanual.com/nervous-system/diseases-of-the-spinal-column-and-cord/toxicoses-of-the-spinal-column-and-cord-in-animals), and [vascular diseases](https://www.msdvetmanual.com/nervous-system/diseases-of-the-spinal-column-and-cord/vascular-diseases-of-the-spinal-cord-in-animals). Many of these diseases are discussed in full in other chapters and are only briefly described here. For a discussion of congenital disorders related to the spinal column and cord,  see [Congenital and Inherited Anomalies of the Nervous System in Small Animals](https://www.msdvetmanual.com/nervous-system/congenital-and-inherited-anomalies-of-the-nervous-system-in-small-animals).
